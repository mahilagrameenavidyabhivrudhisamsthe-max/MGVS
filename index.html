About The Samsthe

Mahila Grameena Vidyabhivrudhi Samsthe is a grassroots organization dedicated to the upliftment and protection of rural women. Rooted in compassion and action, we work closely with women facing domestic abuse, financial instability, social discrimination, and lack of access to education and legal support.

Our mission is simple yet powerful: to ensure every woman, regardless of her background, has the right to safety, self-respect, and opportunity.
